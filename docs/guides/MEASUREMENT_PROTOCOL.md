# Human Measurement Protocol v1.0

**Purpose:** Establish ground truth for AR sizing validation  
**Equipment:** Single measuring tape (fabric, cm markings)  
**Measurer:** Same person for all subjects  
**Date:** January 2026

---

## POSTURE RULES

**Subject must:**
1. Stand upright, shoulders relaxed (no slouching, no exaggerated posture)
2. Arms hanging naturally at sides
3. Feet shoulder-width apart, weight evenly distributed
4. Look straight ahead (neutral head position)
5. Breathe normally (no inhale/exhale holding)
6. Remove bulky clothing (measure over thin shirt/pants only)

---

## MEASUREMENT SEQUENCE

### 1. SHOULDER WIDTH
- **Definition:** Widest point across shoulders (acromion to acromion)
- **Tape placement:** Across back, horizontal line at shoulder bone tips
- **Subject position:** Standing, arms relaxed
- **Reading:** At widest point, tape snug but not tight
- **Record:** To nearest 0.5cm

### 2. CHEST WIDTH
- **Definition:** Torso breadth at nipple line (NOT circumference)
- **Tape placement:** Across chest, horizontal at armpit level
- **Subject position:** Arms slightly away from body
- **Reading:** Front view, widest chest measurement
- **Record:** To nearest 0.5cm

### 3. TORSO LENGTH
- **Definition:** Shoulder top to hip bone
- **Tape placement:** Vertical from shoulder (where neck meets shoulder) to top of hip bone
- **Subject position:** Standing straight
- **Reading:** Natural torso length, no stretching
- **Record:** To nearest 0.5cm

### 4. WAIST (at narrowest point)
- **Definition:** Natural waist circumference at narrowest point
- **Tape placement:** Horizontal around waist, usually above belly button
- **Subject position:** Standing, relaxed abdomen
- **Reading:** After normal exhale, tape parallel to floor
- **Record:** To nearest 0.5cm

### 5. HIP WIDTH
- **Definition:** Widest point across hips (NOT circumference)
- **Tape placement:** Horizontal across widest part of hips/buttocks
- **Subject position:** Feet together, standing straight
- **Reading:** Widest hip measurement from front view
- **Record:** To nearest 0.5cm

### 6. INSEAM
- **Definition:** Inner leg length from crotch to ankle
- **Tape placement:** From crotch seam to inside ankle bone
- **Subject position:** Standing straight, feet slightly apart
- **Reading:** Measure down inner leg to ankle bone
- **Record:** To nearest 0.5cm

## QUALITY CHECKS

**Before recording:**
- [ ] Tape is level (not twisted)
- [ ] Subject in correct posture
- [ ] Measurement point is clear
- [ ] Reading is to nearest 0.5cm
- [ ] Value is within human range

**Human range validation:**
- Shoulder: 35-55cm
- Chest: 30-50cm
- Torso: 45-75cm
- Waist: 60-120cm
- Hip: 30-60cm (width, not circumference)
- Inseam: 65-95cm

**If outside range:** Re-measure, check posture, verify tape placement.

---

## CONSISTENCY RULES

1. **Same tape:** Use only ONE tape for all measurements
2. **Same measurer:** ONE person does all measurements
3. **Same time of day:** Morning preferred (body swelling minimal)
4. **Same room:** Consistent lighting and temperature
5. **No rushing:** Take time to ensure accuracy

---

## ERROR HANDLING

**If measurement seems wrong:**
1. Check posture
2. Re-locate measurement point
3. Measure again (2nd attempt)
4. If still off, note as "uncertain" and flag for review

**Do NOT:**
- Adjust value to "look right"
- Skip measurements
- Change tape mid-study
- Let subject self-measure
